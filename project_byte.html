Project Overview

Detailed technical breakdown of the BYTE quadruped platform development. The core challenge involved managing extreme power spikes and protecting sensitive embedded electronics during startup sequences.

The custom Power Distribution Board (PDB) and Pre-Charge circuit were designed from scratch to handle heavy loads, ensuring safe operation and mitigating inrush currents that could degrade the battery management system.
